# 🚚 Global Supply Chain Intelligence & Logistics Optimization
### An End-to-End Business Intelligence Solution in Power BI

## 📋 Executive Overview

This project involves a comprehensive analysis of DataCo Global’s supply chain operations, covering 180,519 transactions from 2015 to 2018. The primary goal was to identify logistical bottlenecks and revenue-growth opportunities across 5 global markets and 6 departments.

## 🛠️ Technical Highlights

Data Modeling: Implemented a robust Star Schema with 1 Fact table and 5 Dimension tables to ensure high performance across 180k+ rows.

Advanced DAX Engine: Developed 25+ complex measures, including:

Time Intelligence: YoY, MoM, YTD, and 3-Month Rolling averages.

Logistics Analytics: Late Delivery Rate, Avg Days to Ship.

Customer Segmentation.

Prescriptive Features: Engineered a "What-If" Sensitivity Parameter to simulate profit recovery ($3.9M+) through shipping optimization.

UX/UI Design: Utilized Report Page Tooltips with dynamic DAX titles for contextual deep-dives without visual clutter.

## 🔍 Key Business Insights

Logistics Crisis: Identified a 54.8% systemic late delivery rate. Specifically, "First Class" shipping fails its SLA 95.3% of the time.

Revenue Concentration: Europe is the powerhouse market ($9.7M revenue), while the Fishing category dominates sales, generating 3x more revenue than the nearest competitor.

Profitability Leakage: Pacific Asia shows the lowest profit margins (11.5%) due to aggressive discounting in the "Accessories" and "Basketball" categories.

Customer Behavior: The Consumer Segment drives 52% of revenue, but an average of only 3.18 orders per customer suggests a significant opportunity for a loyalty-based retention strategy.

## 🚀 How to Use the Dashboard

Home Page: Navigate through the "Technical Highlights" and high-level KPIs.

Executive Summary: Hover over any Market to see the Top 3 Products and order vs delivery status.

Logistics Page: Use the "Shipping Efficiency Gain" slider to see how reducing late deliveries impacts the bottom line.
